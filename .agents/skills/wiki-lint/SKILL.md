# Wiki Lint

Use this skill to maintain wiki quality.

- Check broken Markdown links, `[[WikiLinks]]`, missing frontmatter, duplicate pages, stale claims, and weak index routes.
- Prefer focused repairs over broad rewrites.
- Append a `lint` or `maintenance` entry to `wiki/log.md`.
